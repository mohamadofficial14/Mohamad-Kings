📜 THE REVOLUTIONARY RULES

  • No Stalemates: We don't do draws here. If the King is captured, it’s FALLMATE. Game over. 👑🏆
  • The Soldier (🛡️): Moves up to 2 squares forward. Captures vertically forward (1 square). No diagonal "shy" moves! 👊
  • The Vice President (💎): When a Soldier reaches the enemy's home row, he eats too much Shawarma, becomes the Vice President, and gains massive power (but is too heavy for horses). 🌯
  • The Car (🏎️): High speed, but respects traffic. Moves up to 5 squares. 💨
  • The Minister (🕌): A spiritual powerhouse. Moves up to 6 squares vertically.
  • The Horse (🐎): The Infinite Galloper. No teleportation limits. Landing a Soldier on a Horse creates a Cavalry Unit (🏇).
  • The King (👑): Majestic but steady. Moves up to 2 squares in any direction.
